## Proof Gap

To turn the sparse rows into a serious obstruction framework, the lab still
needs one of the following:

1. A non-artifact row where every local survivor forces at least two variables
   zero modulo the same prime.
2. A mandatory single-divisor row whose `ell^2` or `ell^3` branch dies, or whose
   later p-adic/LTE analysis forces valuation growth.
3. A proof that the sparse unit-survivor clusters are incompatible with deeper
   lift or modular-shadow consistency constraints.

No Beal proof is claimed.
